Group / church registration — one coordinator, pay later by check or card

Register Your Group

Registering a group of ladies from your church? Enter the coordinator’s contact details, paste your list of attendees, and submit. You’ll get one confirmation email with the total due and a single way to pay — mail one check or use one card link for the whole group. Each lady can add her own meal, dietary, childcare, and seminar choices later using the link in your confirmation.

Coordinator (the person organizing & paying)

Attendees

One lady per line. Use just a name, or First, Last, Email (email optional, but recommended so each lady can complete her own details). You can paste from a spreadsheet, or upload a CSV. Up to 50 per group.

Payment

The whole group pays together — you don’t pay per person now. Pricing is calculated automatically (per attendee) and shown in your confirmation email.
